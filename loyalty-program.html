How exactly does a loyalty program help?

In today’s digital world you are continuously competing for your customer’s attention and loyalty. With data and segmentation technologies, your competitors are able to target your customer base with ads across their social media and search channels at any time of the day. A loyalty program encourages customers to ignore these temptations by showing them how valued they are at your brand.

We're all human and naturally enjoy a little recognition every now and then. If a customer comes back to your salon over and over, they’ll undoubtedly appreciate a little thank you or reward. Recognizing these customers may be easy if you are a small single center but as your brand grows and you hire more team members this can get tricky.

Hence, having a clearly defined program which gives out loyalty points towards products or services is a simple, yet highly effective way of showing this appreciation. The key with giving points is that your most loyal customers are the ones who collect the most points and the occasional guest won’t accumulate many. The whole purpose of this model is to reward your best customers - after all the data shows these are most profitable.

Beyond just thanking customers for their loyalty, the program does serve other purposes. You are also able to collect information on their preferences, likes and dislikes, and personal information which helps you to build a tailored, personalized experience.

Now, when the triggers for them to leave do occur – like a new salon in the neighborhood or the departure of their favorite employee– you don’t immediately lose them. These customers will continue to be loyal to your brand since they are incentivized to continue earning and redeeming points.
