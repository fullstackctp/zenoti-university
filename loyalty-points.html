Creative Ways to Implement Loyalty Points in Your Business

Now, let's get creative with some strategies to get your guests earning loyalty points. Consider these examples...

5 loyalty program ideas

  • Run your loyalty program on your customer mobile app and make it easy for guests to manage their rewards points while boosting app engagement.

  • Offer loyal guests exclusive services or experiences on select days or times in the month (your slow days). Treat them like royalty and earn their loyalty.

  • Appeal to customer values by allowing them to spend their points on a donation to a charitable cause, encouraging them to spend more because their reward points go to a good cause.

  • Offer loyalty points in multiple ways to add value and create a win-win for both the business and the guest. Examples include: referring a friend, booking an appointment online or purchasing high-end products.

  • Boost sales with seasonal loyalty campaigns. For example, Jan to March every $ spent earns more points.
