Elevate Your Brand Through Effective and Meaningful Marketing Campaigns

Marketing is an essential skill for any business owner. Regardless of if you choose to work with a marketing team, hire a marketing manager or do it for yourself, understanding some timeless principles will help you build a better marketing strategy to take your salon or spa to the next level.

Marketing, put simply, is connecting with your ideal customers and letting them know that you’re there to help with their beauty and wellness needs. Say your specialty is getting the perfect icy blonde for your clients, it’s imperative you let the icy blonde hopefuls in your area know that you can help them get the look they’re after. They need you and having the right marketing strategy lets them know where to find you.

Just like having all the right tools for the perfect haircut, this course will fill your marketing toolbox with everything you need to flourish. Starting with the basics of marketing, you’ll discover how to engage with new and existing customers in an effective and meaningful way.

Introduction to Marketing

If you’ve ever gone to a restaurant with a menu that’s way too long and ended up ordering the most basic dish you typically make at home, you’ve experienced decision overwhelm.

>The US hair care industry includes more than 80,000 salons and/or barbershops, so you can imagine how overwhelming it may feel for potential customers to find the right salon - to find YOU.

Don’t be afraid to stand out from the crowd.

Having a quality marketing strategy removes decision overwhelm and drives customers directly to you.

Let’s look at Kylie Cosmetics, for example.

Founded in 2015, the company worth an estimated $900 million. There are a lot of brands that sell lipsticks and lip kits but none as popular as Kylie Cosmetics.

Founded by Kylie Jenner, the company skyrocketed due to Kylie’s social influence, from a wildly popular TV show to a massive Instagram following, Kylie Cosmetics is a prime example of the value and importance of marketing. Essentially, by creating her own line of products, Kylie has taken decision overwhelm away from her followers. If you’re a fan of hers and lipstick, it’s an easy decision to make to pick up her newest lip kit.
