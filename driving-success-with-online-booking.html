Driving Success with Online Booking

These days, online booking is an expectation. Guests want the convenience of being able to access the scheduler anytime, anywhere with the click of a button. Without worrying if their kids are screaming, traffic noises interfering or that they are out of breath from having just got off the treadmill, like you would with a phone call. Almost half of individuals prefer to make a booking outside of business hours. After all, we all think about our to-do list when we are falling asleep don’t we?

Amongst Zenoti customers, 46% of guests prefer to book outside business hours.

What you may not realize is that online booking is also your new best receptionist. Why?

No step is ever missed, making online booking the easier, more efficient process.

“115% increase in average booking price when treatments are booked online” (Zenoti Medspa customer).

Online booking is also proven to improve visit frequency. Amongst Zenoti customers, guests who book an appointment online visit 1 more time per year, or book 10 days sooner than guests who book via phone. One visit may not seem like a lot, but multiple that by the number of different guests that you see in a year, and you are talking about serious revenue.

Additionally, online booking significantly helps to reduce the number of no-shows that affect your business. Particularly if you request credit card information to finalize the booking. Through their partnership with Zenoti, a leading salon customer decreased no-show appointments by 35%, recapturing a little over 1/3 of their bookable appointment blocks.
