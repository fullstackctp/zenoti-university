Why is Guest Retention So Important?

If you’re already providing high-quality procedures, maintenance tips and tricks, and great service, you might be wondering why it’s so important to take additional steps to retain your guests.

Let us explain:

  • Acquiring new customers is expensive.
    •
    • You worked hard to build your current guest base and it likely wasn’t cheap to do so. In fact, it can cost 5x more to acquire a new guest than to retain an existing one. That's why your goal should be to keep customers loyal by offering exemplary guest service and programs that they will find valuable. With only 30% of guests likely to return after their first visit, it’s essential that you employ tools that guarantee their return.
  • Repeat customers boost your bottom line.
    •
    • Did you know that repeat business from happy guests is the biggest contributor to revenue? Our customer data shows that over 77% of a spa or salon’s revenue comes from guests who have visited over four times in a year. Cultivating their loyalty and maximizing their participation in your programs and offerings is important to your business. The more that you can drive their frequency of visit, and get them involved in what you offer, the more committed they'll be to your brand.
  • Your existing guest base is integral to your business ecosystem.
    • Why? Your current guests are some of your brand’s biggest ambassadors. They love their experience with you, and they are not shy to share that experience with their network. That's a huge compliment — but above all, it’s a huge contribution to business growth.
