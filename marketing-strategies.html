Creative Ways to Implement Marketing Strategies

We’ve looked at some examples in previous sections, but seeing to believe is a powerful tool so here are more examples of creative ways to implement marketing strategies in your business:

Build a Blog

Adding a blog or SEO-driven educational content to your website will help you rank higher on Google. It also provides open-source knowledge for your audience and solidifies your brand experience.

Set goals and key content pillars for your blog to ensure it is strategic and adding value to your overall strategy. Consider whether you want to be somewhere that customers come for the tips on caring for their hair or skin or do you want to be a source of industry knowledge and insights. Or even both!

Take a look at the Zenoti blog , we strive to share thoughtful industry insights, tips for salon and spa owners, and updates on our philanthropic work so our audience get to know us better.

Connect with Influencers

According to statistics from Later.com, over the next 6 years social commerce is estimated to increase by 31.4% every year, reaching $604.5 billion (about $1,900 per person in the US) by 2027.

Consider connecting with your current clients or your ideal client on social media for an influencer campaign.

Take Ashley, a beauty influencer for example.

Her reach is over 120K on Instagram and 22K on YouTube. Working with an influencer in your area who has like-minded values can be instrumental to your salon or spa marketing. It’s more than just a numbers game, look to make authentic connections with nano, micro, and/or macro-influencers.

Embrace Email

Your email list is one constant in a world of ever-changing engagement platforms. Your email subscribers are your audience, and they are instrumental to growing your business. Email is a way to engage with your existing client base. And a way to excite potential new clients about connecting with your brand, so plan for a regular cadence of emails.

Zenoti Smart Marketing automates your email marketing efforts by managing your content, target audience segments, timing, and content. This gives you your time back while also eliminating any caps in your appointment book. So, how does it work?

It assesses your daily appointment bookings and runs promotions based on available services and providers. Personalized, promotional emails are then sent out to specific audience segments based on their visit history to encourage them to come in for an appointment.

Utilize Your Website

A leader in trans-racial adoption care hair support, Tutus and Tennis Shoes elevates brand experience through resources and frequently asked questions. The information shared on her site is repurposed across various platforms and she uses a variety of mediums (text, infographic, video) to connect with her audience. Implementing resources like this provides an added benefit to your audience which can lead to increased sales and brand awareness.

Key Takeaways

Build your knowledge of your ideal customer then stand out from the crowd with an intentional marketing strategy and supportive tools like Zenoti.

Get reviews, get noticed, and create a loyal client base to take your salon or spa to new heights.
