Driving growth through Gift Cards

Your location is open, you have the best providers in town, the salon or spa experience you offer is like no other, and you only provided the highest quality service. What’s next?

Gift cards

Did you know the global gift card market was valued at $619.25 billion in 2019? And it’s projected to reach $2,076.51 billion by 2027?

Growing at rate of 16.2% from 2020 to 2027, according to a recent report by Allied Marketing Research, gift cards aren’t just nice to have, they’re a requirement. Businesses such as Starbucks, Target, Macys, Nordstrom’s and even grocery stores have leveraged this growing trend to successfully grow their revenue, brand awareness and customer base. With over 85% of adults shopping online, and with recent travel restrictions due to COVID-19, we have seen an even greater demand for the convenience of the digital gift cards. Meet your guests where they’re at and provide them with what they want by offering a gift card program. In this course you’ll learn why your salon or spa needs to sell gift cards, and how to do it.

Relevance in the Beauty and Wellness Space

Today’s consumer doesn’t want to buy “things,” the want to buy lifestyle experiences.

According to a recent Eventbrite survey:

While experience can cover a broad range of activities, beauty and wellness experiences are amongst the most meaningful that can be accessed by people in a city at almost any time and within easy reach

There’s growing interest in wellness and rejuvenation to combat the stresses and impact of everyday life, and there’s a significant opportunity for salons and spas to capture this market through gift cards.

When your core business is built around making people look and feel good, you’re one of the best places for your guests to buy the gift of experience for their loved ones.
