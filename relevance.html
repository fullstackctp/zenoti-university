Grow Your Customer Base and Increase Revenue with Gift Cards

Your location is open, you have the best providers in town, the salon or spa experience you offer is like no other, and you only provide the highest quality service. What’s next?

Gift cards

Did you know the global gift card market was valued at $619.25 billion in 2019? And it’s projected to reach $2,076.51 billion by 2027?

Growing at rate of 16.2% from 2020 to 2027, according to a recent report by Allied Marketing Research, gift cards aren’t just nice to have, they’re a requirement.

Businesses such as Starbucks, Target, Macys, Nordstrom and even grocery stores have leveraged this growing trend to successfully grow their revenue, brand awareness and customer base.

COVID-19 restrictions have further contributed to the rise of digital gift cards with more individuals identifying them as a convenient gift-giving solution when you can’t shop or visit someone in person.

With over 85% of adults shopping online, it’s time to meet your guests where they’re at and provide them with what they want by offering a gift card program.

In this course you’ll learn why your salon or spa needs to sell gift cards, and how to do it.

Relevance in the Beauty and Wellness Industry

Today’s consumer doesn’t want to buy “things,” the want to buy lifestyle experiences.

According to a recent Eventbrite survey:

While experience gifts can cover a broad range of activities, beauty and wellness experiences are amongst the most meaningful and can be easily accessed by people in a city at almost any time.

There’s growing interest in wellness and rejuvenation to combat the stresses and impact of everyday life, and there’s a significant opportunity for salons and spas to capture this market through gift cards.

When your core business is built around making people look and feel good, you’re one of the best places for your guests to buy an experience gift for their loved ones.

This highlights the impact gift cards are having on their overall revenue and profitability.
