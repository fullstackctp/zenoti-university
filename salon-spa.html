Examples of Successful Salon & Spa Loyalty Programs

For a little additional inspiration, explore the following five examples of successful loyalty programs by fellow beauty and wellness businesses.

Aveda Salon Plus Rewards Program

(One Flat Annual Fee – Unlimited Benefits & Perks)

  • Enroll for a $10, one-time registration fee.
  • Earn points with every Aveda product purchase, everywhere you shop online, in-store and at salons.
  • Exclusive gifts and events
  • Receive a gift and 2x points on your birthday.

Massage Green Spa Loyalty Program

(Earn more perks, when you spend more)

  • Earn a point per dollar spent.
  • Redeem points for free aromatherapy, infrared sauna sessions, facials and massages
  • Double points for product purchases.
  • Additional points for appointments booked online.
  • Additional points for referring a new member.

This is a faster earning rewards program than many others and referring just two friends to start a membership earns you a free one-hour full-body massage.

Massage Envy Spa Loyalty Program

(Monthly Membership to build loyalty)

For a monthly fee, you get a one-hour massage, a total body stretch session or a full-service facial every 30 days plus discounts on added services.

  • Product discounts for members.
  • Membership benefits included at all 1,150 Massage Envy locations.
  • No-risk rollover options.
  • Earn free services with member referrals.

Waxing the City Membership Rewards

(Discount on Services & Products)

Club Orange is the chain’s membership/rewards program, offering:

  • 20% savings on waxing services.
  • 50% off new services.
  • 10% off product purchases.
  • No-risk rollover options.

e.l.f. Beauty Squad

(Tier Reward System -The more you purchase, the better privilege and value you receive for your points)

Elf’s reward system focuses on status, where high-spend guests earn more points and reap exclusive benefits, thus encouraging brand loyalty from their most valuable consumers.

  • Three distinct valuable VIP tiers
    • Glow Getter- 2 points earned for every dollar spent.
      • Perks include enrollment offers, birthday gifts and full-size products redeemable with points.
    • Rising Star- 4 points earned for every dollar spent.
      • Perks include the Glow Getter tier plus $5 coupons, early access to sales and beauty sets redeemable with points.
    • A-Lister- 6 points earned for every dollar spent.
      • Perks include the Rising Star tier plus the ability to choose your own sale day, early access to new products and deluxe beauty sets redeemable with points.

Source - https://www.elfcosmetics.com/e.l.f.-beauty-squad/

Key Takeaways

When it comes to building a loyalty program, creativity and patience are the key to success. Don’t expect to see results overnight but, if implemented correctly, a loyalty program can be a true game-changer for your business by increasing retention rates, boosting profits, and driving guest behavior.
