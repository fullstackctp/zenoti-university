Examples of Successful Salon & Spa Loyalty Programs

1. One Flat Annual Fee – Unlimited Benefits & Perks

This type of model encourages guests to choose you as their one stop shop by enrolling them into a program where they receive exclusive member benefits. Guests feel more value when they shop with you and will choose your business over others because of the investment that they have made.

Here is an example:

  • Enroll for $30.00 (one time-annual fee).
  • Receive free shipping on all your orders.
  • 5 loyalty points earned for every dollar spent.
  • Loyalty points are converted to salon rewards -for every 100 loyalty points earned, receive $5 in salon rewards cash.
  • Receive a birthday gift - on special occasions like your first order and your birthday, you earn double points per dollar.

2. Earn more perks, when you spend more.

Encourage your guests to come in and spend money that will translate into loyalty points. You can earn rewards faster with this program than many others.

Here is an example:

  • Earn a point per dollar spent.
  • Redeem points for service add-ons, or toward service upgrades.
  • Double points for product purchases.
  • 20 points for appointments booked online.
  • 250 points for referring a new member.

3. Monthly Membership to build loyalty.

Monthly memberships support frequency of visit, retention, and brand loyalty. This type of model also provides consistent monthly cash flow into your business.

Here is an example:

  • $50.00 monthly fee.
  • Membership benefits include a free service (amongst a select set), every 30 days.
  • Product discounts for members.
  • No-risk rollover options.

Key Takeaways

When it comes to building a loyalty program, creativity and patience are the key to success. Don’t expect to see results overnight but, if implemented correctly, a loyalty program can be a true game changer for your business by increasing retention rates, boosting profits, and driving guest behavior.
